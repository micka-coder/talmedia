### passuk 3
<b>By most of his brethren.</b> By not by all his brethren, this teaches us that part of the Sanhedrin separated from him because he became close to the monarchy and neglected his studies.<sup>1</sup><i class="footnote">Maseches Megillah 16b and see Rashi there. </i> 
<b>Of all his descendants.</b> This refers back to “of his people,” i.e., to all the seed of his people.
