### passuk 4
<b> Since the contemptibles of Ephraim had said,</b> "What are you ,Gilod?" The lowliest in Ephraim would mock Gilod, saying, "Of what significance are you, Gilod, among Ephraim and among Menasheh?" This is Targum Yonasan's interpretation.

### passuk 5
<b> The Gilodites captured the Yardein crossings.</b> They posted sentries at the crossing areas.
