### passuk 5
<b> The razor [lit. flinger].</b> The razor, as it flings off and casts away the hair.

### passuk 7
<b> Do not consume anything ritually defiled.</b> Anything prohibited to the Nazirite.<sup>2</sup><i class="footnote">Sotah, 9:b. This includes grape extract, in addition to wine. (Rashi, there).</i>
