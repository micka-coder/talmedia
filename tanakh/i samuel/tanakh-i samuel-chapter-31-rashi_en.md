### passuk 12
<b>And burned them there.</b> [Targum renders,] "And they burned for them<sup>1</sup><i class="footnote">I.e., they burned incense to cover the odor and prevent the decay of the bodies.—Radak</i> as they burn for the kings there." As we learned:<sup>2</sup><i class="footnote">A king’s personal belongings are burned at his death to prevent other people from using them, as stated in Maseches Avoda Zarah 11a.</i> We perform the burning [ritual] at [the burial of] kings, and it is not [considered] an Amorite custom.
