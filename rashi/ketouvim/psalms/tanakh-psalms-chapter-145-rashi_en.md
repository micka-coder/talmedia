### passuk 7
<b>Of the remembrance of Your abundant goodness they will speak</b> i.e., one generation to another generation. <b>and they will speak of Your might</b> to each other, saying, “It is good for us to make known to the children of men His mighty deeds and the glory of the majesty of the kingdom of the Holy One, blessed be He.” (I found.) <b>supports</b> (The “nun” is missing from the “aleph-beth” because David saw in it a serious downfall [Amos 5:2]: “The virgin of Israel has fallen and shall not continue to rise.” And he returned and supported her with the holy spirit: The Lord supports all those who fall.)
