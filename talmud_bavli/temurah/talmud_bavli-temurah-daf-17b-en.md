
### Part 1
The Gemara explains that <b>Shmuel</b> could have <b>said to you: Perhaps this</b> <i>tanna</i> <b>also holds</b> that <b>anywhere that</b> an animal <b>is not fit itself</b> for sacrificing, <b>inherent sanctity does not rest upon it.</b> Shmuel himself holds in accordance with those who disagree with this opinion.
